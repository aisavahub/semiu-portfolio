About Me

Dr. Semiu Bello is a visionary academic, dynamic researcher, and respected scholar in media and communication. Hailing from Sagamu, Ogun State, Nigeria,he has risen from humble beginnings to establish himself as a leading voice in his field. His academic journey, marked by resilience and excellence, has earned him five higher degrees between 1997 and 2015, including a PhD from the University of Canterbury, New Zealand—where he made history as the first candidate to complete a PhD in two years in his department.

Dr. Bello’s scholarly focus spans journalism studies, health and development communication, media literacy, communication theories, public relations, and digital media. He has authored over 50 peer-reviewed publications and is widely cited both nationally and internationally. His contributions have earned him editorial and review roles with leading journals, including Global Health Promotion (SAGE), and two international editorial boards in Malaysia and India.

Beyond academia, Dr. Bello is a committed community advocate and youth mobilizer. He is the founder and National Coordinator of The Regenerate Initiative (Tri) for Community Health and Youth Development, and President of Greatways Empowerment Club, a grassroots organization for economic empowerment in Ogun State. A skilled communicator, corporate strategist, and leader, Dr. Semiu Bello is known for his inspiring mentorship, professional speaking, and organizational management. His profile reflects not only academic excellence but also a deep commitment to social impact and youth empowerment.
